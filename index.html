    SYSTEM RULES

    THE DAILY GRIND: You must complete your assigned mission every single day. Completing missions grants EXP to level up and Gold to spend in the Shop.

    THE PENALTY PROTOCOL: If you miss a day, The System will deduct 100 EXP for every day absent. Dropping below your Level's EXP threshold causes Demotion and the permanent loss of Stat Points.

    RED GATES (BOSSES): Every 15 days, a Red Gate spawns. Exercise workloads increase by 15%. Clearing it yields 3X EXP. Failing it drains 100 Gold and triggers the Penalty Protocol.

    SHOP & INVENTORY: Use Gold to buy Elixirs (save your streak), Dungeon Keys (unlock rest days), and Passive Rings. You can also gamble Gold in the Casino.
